The Materials

Sourced from the
World's Finest Mills

Every suit tells a story — of precision, craftsmanship, and timeless style. We craft each piece using the finest fabrics from the world's most renowned mills, including Dormeuil, Reda, Vitale Barberis Canonico (VBC), and StylBiella. These heritage brands represent the pinnacle of luxury, offering exceptional quality, durability, and elegance.

From 9-ounce fresco wools ideal for Austin summers to 14-ounce flannels for cool evenings and formal occasions, we guide every client to the right cloth for the right moment.
