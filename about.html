About Rashaka

Rashaka
Boykins

Entrepreneur & Mobile Clothier · Pearce Bespoke ATX

I'm Rashaka Boykins — an entrepreneur, business IT consultant, and mobile clothier dedicated to helping individuals elevate both their image and their confidence.

Before launching my mobile clothier business, I built my career in IT, where I partnered with organizations to improve operations, streamline processes, and enhance overall performance. That experience sharpened my ability to understand people, identify needs quickly, and deliver tailored solutions that create real impact.

Through business, I learned that success often comes down to how you present yourself — your confidence, your presence, and the impression you leave behind. That realization led me to transition into the world of custom clothing, where I could combine strategy, personalization, and style into a more tangible, personal service. Today, I bring that same consultative approach to every client interaction. I don't just provide clothing — I offer guidance, insight, and a curated experience designed around your goals, lifestyle, and personal brand.

"My mission is simple: to help you show up with intention, confidence, and a look that reflects who you are at your best."

Our Philosophy

What We
Believe

01
Fit above all else
A garment can be made from the finest fabric in the world, but if it doesn't fit, it's worthless. We spend more time on fit than any other element of the craft — because everything else is secondary.
02
Craft, not production
We are not a factory. We don't have quotas. We take on a limited number of commissions at any one time to ensure every client receives our full attention. Slow is smooth, and smooth is excellent.
03
The client is the constant
Trends come and go. Our clients' measurement records, preferences, and style histories stay with us permanently. We build a relationship, not a transaction — and that relationship compounds over time.
04
Community investment
As a Cedar Park-based business, we are committed to bringing world-class custom clothing to the Austin metro — helping individuals from all walks of life show up with confidence and intention. Excellence has no prerequisites — only practice.
The Brand

Pearce Bespoke:
A Legacy of Excellence

Pearce Bespoke is a nationally recognized custom clothing franchise founded on a single principle: that every person deserves the confidence that comes with clothing made specifically for them.

The franchise operates across major US cities, each location independently owned and operated by a master tailor with deep roots in their community. We are not a chain — we are a network of artisans who share a standard.

Mastery
Every franchise owner holds a master tailor credential with minimum 10 years in the craft.
Locality
We serve the community we live in — each location reflects the culture of its city.
Integrity
No hidden costs, no upselling, no compromises on materials. Ever.
Legacy
We create garments meant to be worn for decades and passed on with pride.
